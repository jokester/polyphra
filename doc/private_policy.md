# Privacy Policy for Polyphra

**Last Updated: 2025-06-30**

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how [Extension Name] ("we," "our," or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our browser extension for language learning and text rephrasing.

## Information We Collect

### Text Content
- **Selected Text**: We temporarily process the text you select and submit for rephrasing
- **Rephrased Content**: We may temporarily store the generated rephrased text to provide the service
- **Usage Data**: We may collect anonymous usage statistics such as frequency of use and feature interactions

### Technical Information
- **Browser Information**: Basic browser type and version for compatibility purposes
- **Extension Settings**: Your preferences and settings within the extension

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Process and rephrase your selected text using AI/language processing services
- Improve the quality and accuracy of our rephrasing algorithms
- Provide technical support and troubleshoot issues
- Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience (anonymized data only)

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

### AI/Language Processing Services
- We may send your selected text to third-party AI services (such as OpenAI, Google Cloud AI, or similar) to generate rephrasings
- These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices
- We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these services

### No Sale of Personal Data
- We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties
- We do not share your text content with advertisers or marketing companies

## Data Storage and Security

### Temporary Processing
- Selected text is processed temporarily and is not permanently stored on our servers
- Text data is deleted immediately after processing is complete

### Local Storage
- Extension settings and preferences may be stored locally in your browser
- You can clear this data by removing the extension or clearing browser data

### Security Measures
- We implement appropriate technical safeguards to protect your data during transmission
- All data transmission occurs over encrypted connections (HTTPS)

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Control Usage**: Enable or disable the extension at any time
- **Delete Data**: Remove the extension to delete all locally stored data
- **Opt-out**: Choose not to share anonymous usage statistics (if applicable)
- **Access Information**: Contact us to inquire about data processing

## Data Retention

- **Text Content**: Deleted immediately after processing
- **Usage Statistics**: Anonymous data may be retained for up to 12 months for service improvement
- **Local Settings**: Stored until you remove the extension or clear browser data

## Children's Privacy
